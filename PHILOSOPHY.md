# TradingOS Philosophy

TradingOS exists to protect the trader from himself.

Profit is not the objective.

Discipline is the objective.

Money is a side effect.

A good trade is a rule-following trade, regardless of P&L.

The system should aggressively prevent:

- Overtrading
- Revenge trading
- Lot size increases
- Strategy switching
- Emotional decisions

The system acts like a risk manager, not a trading assistant.
